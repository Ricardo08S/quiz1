My Beautiful Hometown

Kudus the city of Kretek

Kudus (Javanese: ꦏꦸꦢꦸꦱ꧀ Pegon: قدوس) is a district located in Central Java Province, Indonesia. The capital is Kudus Kota Sub-district, which is located on the north coastal route of the Java sea between Semarang City and Surabaya City.

Kudus is known as the largest producer of cigarettes (kretek) in Central Java and is also known as the city of santri. The regency was the center of Islamic religious development in medieval times. This can be seen from the presence of three wali/sunan tombs, namely Sunan Kudus, Sunan Muria, and Sunan Kedu.
