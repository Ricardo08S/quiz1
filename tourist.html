Our Tourist Places

Puteran Hill

The best time to come here is in the morning or afternoon. If you're after sunrise or sunset, you can camp here.

Logung Reservoir

Logung Reservoir, which was built in 2014, has become a favorite destination for many people. You can simply enjoy the beauty of the reservoir or take a boat ride.

Watu Gompeng Waterfall

This tourist destination is still very natural so you have to go through steep roads without safety. However, the beautiful scenery will make you spoil your view. Moreover, when you arrive at the top, you can immediately bathe in a clear and fresh pool.
